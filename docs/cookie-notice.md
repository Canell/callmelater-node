# Cookie Notice

_Last updated: January 2026_

CallMeLater uses cookies and similar technologies **only when strictly necessary** to operate the service. We do **not** use cookies for advertising, tracking, or analytics purposes.

---

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are commonly used to ensure websites function correctly and securely.

---

## 2. Cookies used by CallMeLater

CallMeLater uses **strictly necessary cookies only**, which are required for:

- User authentication and login sessions
- Security (CSRF protection, session integrity)
- Remembering your preferences during an active session

These cookies are essential for the operation of the service and **cannot be disabled**.

---

## 3. Cookies we do NOT use

CallMeLater does **not** use:

- Advertising or marketing cookies
- Third-party tracking cookies
- Behavioral profiling
- Cross-site tracking
- Social media pixels

We do not sell or share cookie data with advertisers or analytics providers.

---

## 4. Legal basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), strictly necessary cookies are permitted based on **legitimate interest**, as they are required to provide the service explicitly requested by the user.

Because CallMeLater only uses strictly necessary cookies, **explicit consent is not required**.

---

## 5. Third-party services

Some third-party services integrated into CallMeLater (such as payment or email providers) may use cookies when you interact directly with their hosted pages (for example, Stripe Checkout). These cookies are governed by the respective providers’ privacy policies.

---

## 6. Managing cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent CallMeLater from functioning correctly.
